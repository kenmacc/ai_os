**Target:** Small business owners in trades, construction, and service industries (UK)
**Offer:** Custom instant quote configurator — €2,500, 1–2 week build
**Goal:** Book a 30-minute discovery call

---

## Usage Notes

- Send emails manually from Gmail — personal tone converts better than automated at this stage
- Space them exactly as marked (Day 1, 3, 7) — no shorter, no longer
- Personalise the first line of Email 1 with something specific about their business if you can (e.g. "Noticed you do bespoke joinery — imagine customers configuring their own piece and getting a price instantly")
- Target: trades, contractors, print shops, furniture makers, signage companies, flooring — anyone with variable-price products or services
- Aim for 20–30 sends per week to start. Track replies, not open rates.

---

## Follow-Up If They Reply With Interest

> "Great — let's get 20 minutes in the diary. I'll ask you a few questions about how your pricing works and we can confirm whether the build makes sense. Here's my calendar: [link]"

## Follow-Up If They Say "Not Right Now"

> "No problem at all. Mind if I check back in a couple of months? Things change fast."
> — Then add to a follow-up list and ping in 6–8 weeks.
