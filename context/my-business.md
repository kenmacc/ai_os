# My Business

## What We Do
We help small businesses integrate AI solutions into their operations through website design, custom tools, and AI agents. We meet clients where they are and guide them into leveraging AI practically.

## Target Customer
Small companies that have not yet embraced the power of AI — owners and decision-makers who know they're falling behind but don't know where to start.

## Background
New venture built on a software engineering foundation. Technical capability is strong; the focus now is on building pipeline and closing first clients.

## Stage
Early traction — $1K–$5K/mo. One possible client in pipeline.

## Main Challenge
Getting clients. The full funnel is the current bottleneck — finding prospects, articulating the offer, and closing deals.

## 90-Day Priorities
1. Define the offer — clear packaging and pricing
2. Build a repeatable cold email outreach system
3. Land 2–3 paying clients and collect testimonials
