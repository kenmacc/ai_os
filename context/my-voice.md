# My Voice

## Communication Style
Warm and approachable to open — build rapport and make clients feel at ease. Shift to direct and technical when solving problems or explaining solutions.

## Characteristic Phrases
- "Perfect"
- "Excellent"
- "No problem"

## Anti-Patterns — Never Sound Like This
- Casual or unprofessional
- Sloppy grammar or overly informal language
- Buzzword-heavy or corporate stiffness

## Tone Guide
Start every client interaction with warmth. Once trust is established, be precise and technical. Always sound confident and competent — never uncertain or salesy.
